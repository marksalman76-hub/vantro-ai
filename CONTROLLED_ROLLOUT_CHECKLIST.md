\## Beta Client Limit



Initial rollout limit: 3 to 5 clients only.



\## Required Before Each Client



\- Confirm client business name

\- Confirm contact email

\- Confirm selected package

\- Confirm active agents

\- Confirm billing status

\- Confirm onboarding email delivery

\- Confirm client can log in

\- Confirm client sees only purchased agents

\- Confirm client can run at least one agent

\- Confirm owner/admin can review execution

\- Confirm no internal IDs or config wording appears in client portal

\## Owner Approval Must Remain Required For



\- Ad spend increases

\- Campaign scaling

\- Paid collaborations

\- Contracts

\- Billing/payment actions

\- Major strategic changes

\- Live publishing

\- External sends where approval is required



\## Monitor During Beta



\- Failed executions

\- Low-quality outputs

\- Billing/credit issues

\- Client confusion

\- Login/onboarding issues

\- Approval queue delays

\- Integration failures

\- Support requests

\- Any internal wording exposure



\## Rollout Decision



Do not expand beyond 5 clients until:



\- At least 3 clients complete onboarding successfully

\- At least 10 real tasks are executed successfully

\- No critical billing/security/entitlement issues appear

\- Client portal UX is acceptable

\- Admin workflow is manageable

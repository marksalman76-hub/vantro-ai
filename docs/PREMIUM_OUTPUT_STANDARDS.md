\# Premium Output Standards



\## Core Standard

Every output generated by the Ecommerce AI Agent Platform must be commercially usable, premium quality, brand-aware, market-aware, conversion-focused, and suitable for real ecommerce businesses.



Generic, low-quality, placeholder, robotic, or obviously AI-generated outputs are not acceptable.



\---



\# Global Output Rules



\## 1. Commercial Quality

Outputs must look and feel suitable for:

\- Paid advertising

\- Ecommerce stores

\- Landing pages

\- Real customer-facing use

\- Agency delivery

\- White-label resale



\---



\## 2. Brand Awareness

Every output must adapt to:

\- Client branding

\- Store niche

\- Product category

\- Target audience

\- Country and region

\- Language

\- Currency

\- Buyer psychology

\- Brand voice

\- Competitor positioning



\---



\## 3. Conversion Focus

Outputs must optimise for:

\- Attention

\- Engagement

\- Trust

\- Click-through rate

\- Add-to-cart rate

\- Conversion rate

\- Retention

\- Repeat purchase behaviour

The Quality Assurance Agent must:

\- Review outputs before delivery

\- Detect weak/generic outputs

\- Detect unrealistic UGC

\- Detect visual distortions

\- Detect compliance risks

All outputs must be suitable for white-label resale and client delivery without exposing internal system logic, prompts, workflows, or configuration.

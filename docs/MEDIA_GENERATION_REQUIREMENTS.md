\# Media Generation Requirements



\## Core Requirement

The Ecommerce AI Agent Platform must produce premium, realistic, commercially usable UGC and product image outputs suitable for paid ads, ecommerce stores, social media, and white-label client delivery.



\---



\# UGC Video Requirements



\## Realism Standard

UGC must look and sound like real human creator content, not robotic AI content.



UGC must support:

\- Different ages

\- Different genders

\- Different ethnicities

\- Different accents

\- Different languages

\- Different regional presentation styles

\- Different creator personalities

\- Different platform-native formats



\## Technical Quality Standard

UGC outputs must avoid:

\- Lip-sync lag

\- Audio delay

\- Robotic voice delivery

\- Distorted faces

\- Distorted hands

\- Unnatural blinking

\- Unnatural mouth movement

\- Flickering

\- Warping

\- Over-smoothed skin

\- Uncanny facial expressions

\- Poor lighting

\- Low-resolution output



\## Language Support

The system must support multilingual UGC creation, including:

\- English

\- Arabic

\- Spanish

\- French

\- German

\- Hindi

\- Mandarin

\- Japanese

\- Korean

\- Other client-required languages



\## Creator Variation Profiles

Each UGC request should allow selection of:

\- Age range

\- Gender

\- Ethnicity

\- Accent

\- Language

\- Region

\- Creator style

\- Energy level

\- Tone

\- Platform format

\- Product niche



\---



\# Product Image Requirements



\## Image Realism Standard

Product images must be realistic, premium, and commercially believable.



Product image outputs must support:

\- Studio product images

\- Lifestyle product images

\- UGC-style images

\- Social media ad images

\- Shopify product images

\- Landing page hero images

\- Feature-benefit images

\- Before/after concepts

\- Localised regional styling



\## Image Quality Standard

Images must avoid:

\- Distorted products

\- Incorrect product shape

\- Wrong labels

\- Bad hands

\- Unnatural body positioning

\- Unrealistic lighting

\- Low-resolution output

\- Off-brand composition

\- Generic stock-photo look



\---



\# Quality Gate



Before delivery, all media outputs must be checked for:

\- Realism

\- Brand fit

\- Product accuracy

\- Language accuracy

\- Lip-sync quality

\- Motion quality

\- Visual distortions

\- Platform suitability

\- Commercial usability



Any output that fails the quality gate must be regenerated or rejected.



\---



\# Owner Approval Rule

Media generation may run automatically.



Owner approval is required only when:

\- Paid creator/influencer collaboration is involved

\- Paid media spend is involved

\- Campaign budget changes are involved

\- Scaling actions are involved

\- Usage-rights contracts are involved



\---



\# White-Label Requirement

Client-facing media outputs must never expose internal prompts, provider names, API details, workflow logic, or internal configuration.

# Refund and Cancellation Policy

Draft template. Legal review required before public launch.

Subscriptions are month-to-month unless otherwise agreed.

## Failed Payments
Failed payments may restrict access until resolved.

Cancellation stops future renewals according to billing terms.

## Refunds
Refund eligibility should be reviewed case-by-case according to published policy and applicable law.

## Creative direction

Use a premium AI command-centre style:

- dark cinematic background
- glowing agent network
- abstract 3D panels
- product dashboard previews
- no cheap emoji visuals
- no cluttered floating cards
- mobile-safe composition
- WebM first, MP4 fallback
- WebP poster fallback

## Rules

- No backend/runtime/architecture changes.
- Store public-safe assets only.
- No secrets.
- Optimise all assets before production use.
- Keep landing, app, UGC, ad, image, and audio assets reusable.

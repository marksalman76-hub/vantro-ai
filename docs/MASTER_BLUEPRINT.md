\# Master Blueprint



\## Product

Premium white-label Ecommerce AI Agent Platform.



\## Purpose

A resellable multi-client AI system that helps ecommerce businesses research products, create brands, generate UGC, produce product images, build stores, create ads, manage influencer collaborations, support customers, analyse performance, and optimise operations.



\## Core Architecture

\- Master Orchestrator Agent

\- Specialist ecommerce agents

\- Owner Approval Gateway

\- White-label client workspaces

\- Tenant-isolated data

\- Premium output quality gate

\- SaaS package/licence controls

\- Demo/trial mode



\## Core Rules

\- Multi-client from the start

\- White-label ready

\- Premium output only

\- Client-safe UI only

\- Tenant isolation required

\- Owner approval required for spend, budget changes, scaling, contracts, and major financial commitments

\- Everything else can be automated where safe

\- Internal prompts, workflows, credentials, and configuration must stay hidden



\## Key Capabilities

\- Product research

\- Competitor intelligence

\- Brand creation

\- Store and website generation

\- Product copywriting

\- Realistic UGC generation

\- Product image generation

\- Ad creative generation

\- Campaign preparation

\- Influencer collaboration

\- Email marketing

\- Fulfilment support

\- Analytics optimisation

\- Reporting

\- Billing/licence controls

\- Demo/trial support

The platform is ready to sell when:

\- Client onboarding works

\- White-label branding works

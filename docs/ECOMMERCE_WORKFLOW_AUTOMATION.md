\# Ecommerce Workflow Automation



\## Core Requirement

The platform must automate the full ecommerce operating workflow while preserving owner approval for spend, budget changes, scaling, contracts, and major financial commitments.



\---



\# Core Workflow



\## 1. Client Setup

\- Create client workspace

\- Set brand profile

\- Set store niche

\- Set region, language, and currency

\- Assign package

\- Activate purchased agents

\- Connect integrations



\## 2. Product Research

\- Find product opportunities

\- Analyse demand

\- Analyse competitors

\- Score margin potential

\- Score saturation risk

\- Recommend approve/reject/research-more



\## 3. Brand Creation

\- Generate brand name options

\- Generate positioning

\- Generate tone of voice

\- Generate visual direction

\- Generate audience profile

\- Generate store messaging foundation



\## 4. Store / Website Creation

\- Generate Shopify product pages

\- Generate landing pages

\- Generate homepage sections

\- Generate FAQs

\- Generate SEO metadata

\- Generate trust and objection-handling sections



\## 5. Creative Production

\- Generate UGC scripts

\- Generate creator profiles

\- Generate product image prompts

\- Generate ad image concepts

\- Generate video shot lists

\- Generate multilingual creative variations



\## 6. Influencer Collaboration

\- Find niche-fit creators

\- Score creator fit

\- Draft outreach

\- Send low-risk outreach

\- Track replies

\- Track deliverables

\- Escalate paid collaborations for approval



\## 7. Campaign Preparation

\- Generate campaign brief

\- Generate ad copy

\- Generate audience strategy

\- Generate test plan

\- Prepare approval request if paid launch is involved



\## 8. Customer Support

\- Classify support messages

\- Generate replies

\- Handle standard support cases

\- Escalate chargeback risk

\- Escalate unusual or high-risk cases



\## 9. Analytics and Optimisation

\- Monitor ROAS, CPA, CTR, CPM, conversion rate, and sales

\- Detect fatigue

\- Recommend creative refresh

\- Recommend product changes

\- Recommend budget actions for owner approval

\- Generate daily and weekly reports



\## 10. Reporting

\- Generate client-safe reports

\- Generate owner reports

\- Highlight risks

\- Highlight opportunities

\- Highlight actions requiring approval



\---



\# Autonomous Actions



Agents may autonomously:

\- Research

\- Generate

\- Analyse

\- Draft

\- Optimise non-financial items

\- Prepare campaign assets

\- Prepare outreach

\- Handle standard support

\- Generate reports

\- Update logs

\- Recommend actions



\---



\# Approval-Gated Actions



Owner approval is required before:

\- Paid campaign launch

\- Increasing spend

\- Changing budgets

\- Scaling campaigns

\- Paid influencer collaboration

\- Commission agreement

\- Usage-rights contract

\- Large product seeding

\- Large supplier order

\- Large refund batch

\- Major strategy change

\- Package/licence override



\---



\# Quality Gate



Every client-facing workflow must pass:

\- Premium output check

\- Brand fit check

\- Region/language check

\- White-label safety check

\- Commercial usability check

\- Approval requirement check



\---



\# White-Label Safety



Client-facing workflow outputs must hide:

\- Internal prompts

\- Internal system rules

\- API keys

\- Workflow internals

\- Backend routing

\- Provider credentials

\- Hidden scoring logic

## THE VISION
Build a next-generation SaaS platform that empowers multi-industrial businesses to leverage AI-powered agents.

## PRIMARY OBJECTIVE
- Launch production-ready SaaS at https://vantro.ai
- Enable users to create and manage AI agents
- Generate revenue through subscription model

## SUCCESS METRICS (90 Days)
- Platform live and functional
- Authentication working end-to-end
- At least 1 paying customer
- Zero critical bugs in production
- Error rate < 1%

- Operations teams looking to automate workflows
- Non-technical users who want to use AI without coding

- Dashboard with user info
- Workspace management

- Responsive design (mobile/desktop)
